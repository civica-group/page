About Civica News

Civica News is an independent left-wing publication based in Costa Blanca. We serve intellectuals, students, and organised labour — those who seek honest, rigorous journalism unconstrained by commercial pressures.

The publication is privately owned and funded through interest generated from the assets of the Civica Trust Group, ensuring full editorial independence.

Our Editions

  • Civica Live — Live television format content posted on our platforms.
  • Civica Now — Live, brief headlines available on our independent platforms.
  • Civica Review — In-depth reviews into a specific subject.
  • Civica Standard — Regular articles about topics in the headlines.
  • Civica Special — Tabloid style articles available on our platforms and Civica Live Special.

Editorial Independence

Civica operates without advertising revenue, political party funding, or corporate ownership. Our editorial decisions are made entirely by our staff and guided by our commitment to accuracy, rigour, and the public interest.
