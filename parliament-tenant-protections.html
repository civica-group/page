Regional Parliament Votes to Extend Tenant Protections

New legislation will cap rent increases at inflation for the next three years.

Civica Now · Costa Blanca Friday, 22 May 2026

The regional parliament voted 47 to 31 this afternoon to extend tenant protection legislation, capping annual rent increases at the official inflation rate for a minimum of three years.

The bill, introduced by the left-nationalist bloc and supported by two independent representatives, passed on its second reading after three months of committee debate.

Landlord associations have announced their intention to mount a legal challenge. The new protections are expected to come into force within sixty days.
